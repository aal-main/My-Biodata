Assalamu Alaikum — A Journey Toward a Blessed Union

Personal Introduction

My name is Abdullah Al Main, a 26-year-old Muslim Bangladeshi from Barishal, born on 11th October 2000. Standing at 5'5" with a humble nature and a grateful heart, I believe in living a life guided by simplicity, sincerity, and faith. My blood group is O+, and I strive to carry a calm and optimistic spirit in every chapter of life.

Family Background

I belong to a loving nuclear family that beautifully balances religious values. My respected father, Md. Bahauddin, is a retired service holder, and my beloved mother, Dil Afroza, is a dedicated homemaker. I am blessed with four brothers and one sister — among them, 1st one Abdullah Al Sharif, serves as a lecturer at Government Teacher's training College, Barishal. While the 2nd one Abdullah Al Maruf, is a cash officer in Janata Bank. The 3rd one Abdullah Al Masum, is a Student of Development Studies, University of Chittagong. The 4th one is me, Abdullah Al Main, a student of Mathematics Department, University of Chittagong. The last member of our family is my sister, Sabiha Nusrat, a student of Department of Philosophy, University of Chittagong. We currently reside in Narayanganj (rented), though our hometown is Barishal.

Educational Journey

My educational path has been one of dedication and purpose. I completed my SSC in 2017 from Govt. Hazi Ibrahim Alamchan Model School & College, Bandar, Narayanganj and HSC in 2019 from Govt. Tolaram College, Naarayanganj, later graduating with an Honours degree in Mathematics from the University of Chittagong (2024). At present, I am pursuing my MS in Applied Mathematics from the same esteemed institution, aspiring to grow both intellectually and spiritually.

About Me — The Person I Am

At heart, I am a simple, calm, and friendly person who finds deep joy in life’s small blessings. I try to approach life with gratitude, understanding, and patience. Though I am striving to become more consistent in my prayers, my faith remains the quiet foundation of my life — rooted in moderation, humility, and sincerity.

Hobbies & Interests

I find peace in long walks and drenching in the rain. I enjoy playing, spending quality time with family and friends, and engaging in meaningful conversations. My curiosity extends to Islamic literature, history, geopolitics, politics, and technology — as I believe knowledge deepens one’s understanding of the world and faith.

Location Preference
